Hosted temporary sites vs self-hosted lab

A TasteWP alternative with lifetimes you control.

Choose TasteWP when you want a hosted WordPress site immediately, including a free temporary option with no server setup. Choose SpawnWP when you want reusable environments, storage and lifetime policy on a server you operate.

Competitor information checked July 13, 2026
The central trade-off

Immediate hosted access or private infrastructure.

TasteWP creates hosted WordPress test sites with selectable WordPress and PHP versions, plugins, themes and templates. Its official FAQ currently lists free sites for two days without login or seven days when logged in; premium sites remove expiry.

SpawnWP needs one server installation first. After that, the cockpit creates isolated sites that are permanent by default or expire on the 1, 3, 7 or 30-day lifetime you select.

Common questions

Before trading immediacy for ownership.

Do SpawnWP sites expire automatically?

Only if you choose a lifetime of 1, 3, 7 or 30 days. Sites are permanent by default.

Is SpawnWP as instant as TasteWP?

No. TasteWP can create a hosted temporary site without server setup. SpawnWP first requires a supported server, DNS and installation.

Does self-hosted mean unlimited?

No. SpawnWP does not impose a SaaS plan, but sites remain limited by your server capacity, storage and administration.
