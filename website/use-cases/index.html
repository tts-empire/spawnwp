A clear boundary

Development, testing and demos — not production hosting.

SpawnWP keeps a remotely accessible lab running on your server. It does not manage production traffic, client billing, continuous staging sync or anonymous public provisioning.

Bring a fresh server and two hostnames.

SpawnWP configures Docker, Nginx, TLS and the authenticated cockpit; you remain responsible for the infrastructure.
